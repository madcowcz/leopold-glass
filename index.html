About Leopold Glass

Leopold Glass (Sklo Leopold s.r.o.) is a specialist glass processor based in the Czech Republic with over a decade of experience in safety and technical glazing.

Our core capabilities include VSG lamination, slumping, bending, and custom safety glazing for industrial applications. Machine viewing windows are a regular part of our production, supplied to machine tool manufacturers across the EU.

We work directly — no intermediaries — which means shorter lead times and straightforward communication.
